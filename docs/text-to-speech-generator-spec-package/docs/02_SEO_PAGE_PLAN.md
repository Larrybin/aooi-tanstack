## Trust Pages

- `/pricing/`
- `/faq/`
- `/privacy-policy/`
- `/terms-of-service/`

## Page Template Requirements

Each SEO page must include:

1. Unique title
2. Unique meta description
3. Unique H1
4. One intro paragraph specific to the intent
5. Generator component
6. Page-specific sample text
7. Feature bullets relevant to the page intent
8. FAQ block with 4-6 unique questions
9. Internal links to related pages
10. Canonical URL

## Internal Linking

Homepage should link to:

- Free Text to Speech
- Read Aloud
- Text Reader
- AI Text to Speech
- TTS Online
- Text to Audio
- Pricing

Every SEO page should link back to `/` and `/pricing/`.

## Deferred SEO Scope

Do not create in v1:

- Language pages
- PDF to Speech
- Document Reader
- Article to Audio
- Study Notes to Speech
- Accessibility Text Reader
- Dyslexia Text Reader
- Competitor alternative pages
