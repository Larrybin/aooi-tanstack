Text to Speech Generator is provided for legitimate reading, study, accessibility, review, and productivity workflows.

Do not use the service for impersonation, scams, fraud, illegal activity, hate or harassment, sexual exploitation, deepfake or misleading content, or infringement of third-party rights.

You are responsible for the text you submit and the audio you generate.

## Plans and Limits

Free, lifetime, and extra-credit plans include explicit character limits, request limits, history limits, and retention periods. Lifetime plans are not unlimited generation plans.

Guest previews do not include MP3 downloads. MP3 downloads require sign-in.
