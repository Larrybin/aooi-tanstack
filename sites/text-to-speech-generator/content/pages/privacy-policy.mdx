# Privacy Policy

Text to Speech Generator converts submitted text into speech and stores only the data needed to provide previews, downloads, account history, quotas, billing, and abuse prevention.

## Text and Audio

Generated history stores the first 100 characters of submitted text, character count, language, voice, model, request hash, audio object key, generation time, and expiry time. Full original text is not retained in product history.

Generated audio is stored only for the retention period attached to the active plan. Expired audio may be deleted and cannot be downloaded.

## Account and Payment Data

Sign-in data is handled by the existing aooi authentication system. Paid purchases are processed by Creem. We store the billing status, order identifiers, entitlement state, quota state, and credit ledger entries needed to operate the service.
