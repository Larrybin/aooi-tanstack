# Terms of Service

MP4 Compressor is provided for legitimate video compression workflows, including sharing, storage, and upload-size reduction.

## Acceptable Use

You must own or have permission to process the MP4 files you use with this tool. Do not use the service for illegal activity, infringement, or deceptive workflows.

## Browser Processing

The MVP runs video compression in your browser. Large files may be slow or fail on low-memory devices.
