# Privacy Policy

MP4 Compressor compresses videos locally in your browser for the MVP workflow.

## Video Files

Selected MP4 files are processed on your device. They are not uploaded to a backend server for compression.

## Local Processing

Temporary browser object URLs and in-memory processing data are used only to preview and compress the selected video during the current session.

## Contact

Questions can be sent to support@mp4compressor.example.com.
