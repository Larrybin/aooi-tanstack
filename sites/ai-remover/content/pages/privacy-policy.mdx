## Introduction

AI Remover helps users remove unwanted objects, people, and background distractions
from photos. This policy explains what information we collect, how uploaded images
are handled, and how long files are kept.

By using AI Remover, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account information

If you create an account, we collect the name, email address, profile image, and
authentication details needed to operate your account.

### Uploaded images and masks

When you use the object remover, we process the images you upload, the mask you
paint, and the generated result. These files are used to provide the removal
workflow, show your result, support downloads, and power your image history when
you are signed in.

### Usage and quota information

We track product usage, quota reservations, completed jobs, failed jobs, download
events, anonymous session identifiers, and abuse-prevention signals such as IP
rate-limit counters.

### Payment and subscription information

Paid subscriptions are processed by Creem. We store billing status, plan
information, subscription identifiers, and payment event records needed to keep
your AI Remover entitlements in sync. We do not store full card numbers.

### Device and cookie information

We may use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, session
continuity, abuse prevention, analytics, and product operation.

## How Uploaded Images Are Used

Uploaded images are used only to provide AI Remover functionality:

- storing the original image
- storing the mask image
- submitting the image and mask to an AI processing provider
- storing the generated result and thumbnail
- displaying and downloading your result
- showing signed-in users their recent image history

We do not use uploaded images to train AI models.

## Image Retention

Default retention windows are:

- Guest images: 24 hours
- Free signed-in user images: 7 days
- Pro and Studio user images: 30 days

After the retention period, images are eligible for automatic deletion. Signed-in
users can also manually delete their own images from the image history when that
feature is available.

Deleted or expired images should no longer be accessible through AI Remover.

## How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate AI
Remover, including:

- authentication providers
- email providers
- payment provider Creem
- storage infrastructure
- AI image processing providers
- analytics and abuse-prevention services

These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services
to AI Remover.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data and
uploaded images. No internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

## Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for uploading only images that you own or have permission to
process. Do not upload images if doing so would violate another person's privacy,
copyright, trademark, or other rights.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated terms will be posted
on this page with a new effective date.
